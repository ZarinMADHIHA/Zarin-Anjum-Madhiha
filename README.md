### 👩‍🚀 About Me

I am an Electrical Engineering graduate and independent researcher specializing in **Planetary Exploration**, **Robot Perception**, and **Embedded AI**. My work focuses on developing resource-efficient systems for extreme environments and accessible healthcare.

I co-developed **L-VITeX**, a lightweight terrain navigation system featured by Hackster.io, and **TinyExo**, a TinyML solution reducing prosthetic costs by 80%. I also research Digital Twin technologies for space debris tracking and manipulator control. Previously, I co-led my university's Mars rover team to **11th place globally** at the International Rover Design Challenge 2023. I am currently pursuing my MSc at BRAC University.

---

### 🔬 Research Interests

- **Planetary Exploration:** Autonomous navigation for Mars rovers.
- **Robot Perception:** SLAM, 3D Reconstruction, and Semantic Mapping.
- **Edge AI:** TinyML for resource-constrained environments.
- **Digital Twins:** Simulation-to-reality transfer for manipulators and satellites.

### 🚀 Featured Projects

#### [L-VITeX: Light-Weight Visual Intuition for Terrain Exploration](Link-to-Repo)
*August 2024 - Sept 2024*
A **TinyML-based** approach for terrain exploration aimed at resource-constrained explorers.
- Achieved **99% ROI detection accuracy** with < 200 KB peak RAM usage.
- Implemented 3D reconstruction using monocular camera-based Gaussian Splats.
- **Tech:** Edge Impulse, Python, C++.

#### [Cost-effective TinyML-Based Gait Diagnosis Unit (TinyExo)](Link-to-Repo)
*Jan 2023 - Nov 2023*
A low-cost wearable unit for robotic prostheses using an **ESP32 NodeMCU**.
- Classified 5 gait scenarios via IMU data with **92% accuracy**.
- Reduced processing unit cost by up to **80%**.
- **Tech:** Edge Impulse, Embedded Systems, DSP.

#### [Digital Twin-Aided Space Debris Detection](Link-to-Repo)
*June 2024 - Sept 2024*
Multi-modal debris detection system for multi-satellite architectures.
- Leveraged **NVIDIA Isaac Sim** for real-time simulation and predictive modeling.
- Enhanced collision avoidance strategies through sensor fusion.
- **Tech:** ROS2, Isaac Sim, Python.
